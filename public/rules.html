📖 Game Rules

← Back

Quiz

  • Each player votes for 3 categories before the round starts.
  • Answer each question before time runs out.
  • Correct answers give points. Faster correct answers rank higher.
  • A reveal shows the correct answer, your points, rank, and total score.
  • Highest total score at game over wins.

Shithead

  • Swap phase: arrange your 3 face-up cards before play starts.
  • Play cards of equal or higher rank than the current top card.
  • Special cards can reset or clear the pile based on game rules.
  • If you cannot play, pick up the pile.
  • The first player to get rid of all cards wins.

If your host uses house rules, the host's call applies for this room.
